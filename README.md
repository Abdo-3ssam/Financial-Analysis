## üìà Advanced Financial KPIs Tracked
In this dashboard, we don't just look at numbers; we track **Financial Health Metrics**:

* **Burn Rate:** How fast capital is depleted across subcategories.
* **SaaS Density:** Percentage of income going to digital subscriptions (Adobe, Microsoft, etc.).
* **Essential vs. Discretionary:** The ratio between "Food/Utilities" and "Entertainment".
* **Volatility Index:** Identifying which categories (like Transport or Groceries) fluctuate most.
